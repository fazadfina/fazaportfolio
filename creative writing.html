The Unpainted Canvas

By Faza Dafina Putri Sarma

Amid the silence of her heart, confusion whispers to this womanâ€™s soul.
In the story of love, her doubts are like a canvas yet to be painted.
Her steps pause in the maze of uncertainty, trapped within the depths of her heart.
Like the full moon, doubts creep in, haunting and grounding her restless soul.
Each thread of her thoughts ends in a knot of uncertainty that blinds her heart.
Obstacles are relentless, like a river that never ceases to flow.
Love becomes a heavy question mark in her mind, weighed down by the unease that leaves her restless and uneasy.
Step by step, each moment is a lesson in dancing through the maze of doubt.
Seeking answers behind the blurred shadows that spin her mind.
Doubt fills every path; no certainty touches the depths of her heart.
It feels like lightning strikes her spirit, yet inside, she remains silent.
In the end, there comes a glimmer of clarity.
Every doubt is an ironic entanglement.
In her story, certainty is etched within her.
The woman, lost in her confusion, finds clarity within the sprawling doubt.
